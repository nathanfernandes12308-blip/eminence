What We Are

Not a guild that waits for war.

Hogyoku Vanguard carries two halves on purpose. 鏡花水月 is the old phrase for something too fine to hold — a reflection, a season, a rank. Vanguard is the line that holds it anyway. Everything we run, our drafting, our rosters, our war calendar, exists to keep both halves true at once.

We didn't take the #1 Global seat by showing up loudest. We took it by showing up every season, with a roster that studies the enemy's pattern before the enemy studies ours. Five war teams, each running their own formation. One claim to the top that nobody has managed to take back.
